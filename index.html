About me

Quick learner, adaptable, determined...

... those are some of the adjectives people tend to use to describe me. I kind of agree. I am all those things and then some (says humbly).

I'm an Industrial Engineer with a Master of Science in Supply Chain and Lean Management. I have over six years of experience in manufacturing and services environments.

Originally from Chihuahua, Mexico, some of my academic and career choices have taken me to Texas and Illinois, USA, and even all the way to Toulouse, France. These international experiences have definitely helped mold the person that I am today.

I've been interested in technology, particularly computers and programming, for as long as I can remember, but I had never done anything about it. It only took a world pandemic for me to actually scratch that itch and I decided to learn to code. I took a break from my management consulting job, I found the Le Wagon Full-Stack Development bootcamp and I enrolled in the Mexico campus with the hopes that my brain would be able to process the very intense load for 9 whole weeks. It turns out my brain is really good at it! (says humbly, again).

During the bootcamp I learned Ruby on Rails, HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript, I also dipped my toes on relational databases, PostreSQL, bootstrap, jQuery, and heroku. I oficially became a Full-Stack web developer in December 2020, which I think is a very nice way to close this weird year.

I'm hoping that all of the knowledge I now have will open some pretty fun doors for me. Still reading? I guess that means you're really into my story, you should contact me to learn even more üòÅ.
